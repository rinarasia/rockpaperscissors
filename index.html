Welcome to the Stone Age!

Pterra has challenged you to a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors to determine who will be named Supreme Dinosaur.


To play, click on an object (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) to play that object against Pterra. If your choice beats Pterra, you win a point! If your choice loses to Pterra, Pterra wins a point. If you and Pterra choose the same object, the round ends in a draw.


In order to win the game, you must earn 5 Points before Pterra does.
